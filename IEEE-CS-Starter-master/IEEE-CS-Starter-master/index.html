WHO WE ARE

Sri Sairam Engineering College

Sri Sairam Engineering College, Chennai, established in the year 1995 by MJF.Ln.Leo Muthu, Chairman of Sapthagiri Educational Trust, is a non-profitable, and non-minority institution. A well defined vision, highly committed mission and dedicated leadership facilitate Sri Sairam Engineering College to be in the best of educational institutions in the country. Since its inception , our Institution has grown into a vast conglomerate of magnificent buildings, state-of-the art and sophisticated laboratories internet centres, modern library and a superlative sports complex-each a land mark in itself across 300 acres. Its quiet and idyllic surroundings, comprising of the architecturally and aesthetically designed buildings, the sports playgrounds and the lush greenery make it one of the most preferred destinations for the aspirants of Engineering studies.

IEEE

IEEE, an association dedicated to advancing innovation and technological excellence for the benefit of humanity, is the world's largest technical professional society. It is designed to serve professionals involved in all aspects of the electrical, electronic amd computing fields and related areas of science and technology that underlie modern civilization. IEEE's roots go back to 1884 when electricity began to become a mojor influence in society. There was one major established electrical industry, the telegraph, which since the 1840s had come to connect the world with a data communications system faster than the speed of transportation. The telephone and electric power and light industries had just gotten underwway.

IEEE COMPUTER SOCIETY INAUGURATION

IEEE COMPUTER SOCIETY - students branch in Sri Sai Ram engineering college was inaugurated on 17th February 2017 for opening the door to opportunities and for developing the professional identities of the students.

Educational Outreach Programme - I

Industrial Visit to NLC limited

Industrial Visit Program to TCS

As a part of the IEEE-SB activities an industrial visit was organised to TCS (Siruseri, Chennai) on 17th July 2017. The main objective of the program was educating the students about the current trends in IT sector and to create awareness among all the students about the IT Industry and its working sector.

Educational Outreach Program - II

Awareness Programme on IEEE

To create an awareness about IEEE among the young buds, the IEEE-SB students chapter took an initiative by organizing an awareness program about IEEE on 8th August 2017 for the second year students of the all branches.

IEEE DAY-2017

IEEE day is a day in which the engineers and IEEE members will gather together to share their technical ideas. On 4th October 2017, IEEE day was celebrated with the theme of “Leveraging Technology for a Better Tomorrow” and some contests like technical quiz, technical connections and an online event (doodle art) were also conducted for all the branch students.
